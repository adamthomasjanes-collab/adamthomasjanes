This portfolio is intentionally organized as Issue No. 01: a digital publication rather than a conventional portfolio menu.

## Navigation / Table of Contents

## Publication Sequence

Education & Recognition / Awards & Publications — supporting spread, unnumbered

## Naming Rules
- Navigation labels remain short enough to scan in the running header.
- On-page headings may use fuller editorial names such as “Reference Library” and “Career Timeline.”
- Feature overlays navigate by “Previous Feature / Next Feature.”
- Archive overlays navigate by “Previous Issue / Next Issue.”

## Numbering Rule
Navigation acts as the table of contents. Large translucent ghost numbers mark major publication chapters. Education & Recognition and Awards & Publications share one supporting spread and remain unnumbered. The final ghost number, 08, belongs to the Colophon and matches its navigation and visible on-page heading.
